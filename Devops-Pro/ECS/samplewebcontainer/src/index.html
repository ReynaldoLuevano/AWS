About Us

We are a new player but we have years of experience

Our history


Jumping Brains was founded in September 2023. Initially as a side job when Reynaldo Luévano became an AWS Authorized Instructor. We started delivering several AWS Courses to several companies of different sectors.

In November 2025, Reynaldo took the decision to focus all the time in energy on this idea with the objective that Jumping Brains becomes one company that offers IT Solutions under one philosophy "Keep It Simple & Raise the bar"

We provide more than 20 years of experience on IT , leading projects for companies from different sectors of the industry such as Banking, Insurance, Telecommunications and Small Enterprises. Recently we have delivered AWS courses as part of a program sponsored by the Community of Madrid, Spain
